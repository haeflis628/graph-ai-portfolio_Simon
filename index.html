About the course

An 8‑week live cohort on no‑code AI tools for responsible task automation — spanning writing, analysis, text mining, custom GPTs/chatbots, workflow automation, and research communication.

Bootcamp features

Weekly workshops and help sessions, private community forums, personalized feedback, capstone coaching, a shareable certificate, and lifetime tutorial access.

Live workshops Help sessions Capstone coaching

Grading rubric

Online quizzes (15%), workshop assignments (35%), attendance & participation (10%), final project (40%). Passing grade: 80% (with attendance expectations).

Quizzes Assignments Final project

Modules (high‑level)

A compact view of what we cover — designed for practical, responsible use in real work and research.

Module 1

Websites, documents & presentations (Markdown/LaTeX/HTML, simple research sites, AI‑assisted slides).

Module 2

AI‑automated data analysis (datasets, dashboards, batch file processing, automated reporting).

Module 3

GenAI for research & text analysis (literature review, classification, synthesis workflows).

Module 4

Custom GPTs & chatbots (RAG, knowledge bases, integrations, Botpress workflows).

Module 5

AI for task automation (agents, email workflows, app‑to‑app orchestration, browser control).

Module 6–7

Communication & outreach → capstone project & showcase (responsible dissemination, final presentations).
